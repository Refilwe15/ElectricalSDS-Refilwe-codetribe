OSHA Electrical Safety - SDS(Safety Data Sheet)

Common Electrical Hazards And Preventive Steps

Avoid working on wet areas because water is electro conductor and you can also trip and fall

Do not work with energized circuits because if not handled properly it might cause severe injury or death

Remove jewellery before working with electricity because jewellary is a conductor

Wear appropriate personal protective equipment(PPE) before working with electricity to protect your self.
